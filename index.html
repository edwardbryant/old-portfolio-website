Hi, I'm Edward Bryant, a Chicago-based web developer and designer interested in making finely crafted websites that are intuitive to use. I prefer to write in JavaScript, CSS, jQuery, Python, and PHP, but I have had experience with a number of other languages and frameworks. - Although I gradulated law school I have recovering lawyer transitioning my freelance - former art major and award-winning cartoonist.
