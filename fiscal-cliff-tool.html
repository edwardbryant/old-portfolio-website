• 2013 Fiscal Cliff Estimator •

2012
HTML • CSS • Bootstrap • JavaScript
This was a small web-based app I built while at Wolters Kluwer. The app was proposed and built within a few days to provide customers with guidance during the heat of the federal credit limit negotiations at the end of 2012 (i.e., the “Fiscal Cliff”). The app models the impact of various legislative proposals on specific taxpayers. The release of the tool even got some media coverage in Accounting Today.
