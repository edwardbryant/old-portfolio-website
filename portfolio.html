• About Me •

I am a Chicago-based full stack web developer who is currently focused on JavaScript and Python. After law school and several years working in the legal and tax industry, where my role partially included web development, I am looking to move into a full-time developer role where I can further hone my skills. Through my past work experience, online courses, as well as freelance and personal projects, I have had years of experience with both frontend technologies (HTML5, CSS3, SASS, JavaScript, jQuery) and back-end technologies (Python, MySQL, PostgreSQL, XML/XSLT). I have also worked with numerous other frameworks and libraries (Bootstrap 2/3, Underscore.js, Flask/SQLAlchemy, NLTK). I have recently taken Thinkful's Frontend Developer Program and Udacity's Full Stack Nanodegree Program. I am especially interested in open source technologies, natural language processing, and web accessibility.
